Purdue Indie Game Developers' Club

About the Club


We're a small, but close-knit club that first got together in Spring 2015 and became the Independent Game Developers Club in Fall 2015. We want a place where we can work on our own games, but can ask the person sitting next to us for help, ideas, feedback, and more. That's what we do. We host a hub of independent game developers.
